# Privacy Policy — Chingu

**Last updated: May 3, 2026**

## What data Chingu collects

Chingu records browser interactions (clicks, page state) and captures screenshots of web pages during workflow recording. This data is used solely to generate SOP guides.

## Where data is stored

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in `storage` API. Nothing is sent to any external server. There is no backend, no cloud sync, no analytics, and no telemetry.

## What data is NOT collected

- No personally identifiable information
- No authentication credentials
- No financial information
- No location data
- No browsing history outside of active recording sessions

## Third parties

Chingu does not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties.
