# Chingu (친구)

The open source Chrome extension for SOPs that actually work.

## The problem, in numbers

| Tool | Price | #1 complaint on G2 |
|------|-------|---------------------|
| Scribe | $13/seat/mo (5-seat minimum) | "Screenshots come out blank" |
| Tango | $22/seat/mo | "Doesn't capture typed text" |
| SweetProcess | $99/mo flat | "No auto-capture at all" |
| MagicHow | $10/seat/mo | "Limited integrations" |

The SOP software market is worth **$5.85 billion**.
6 major Chrome extensions. 15 recurring pain points across G2, Capterra, and Reddit.
Zero solve all of them.

The #1 complaint across every tool? **The screenshots don't work.**

A 10-person SMB team pays $130-260/month for SOP tools that can't reliably
take a screenshot. That's broken.

## What Chingu does

One job. Capture browser workflows into clean, shareable step-by-step guides.

- Click record. Use any website. Click stop.
- Get a step-by-step guide with screenshots **that actually work**.
- Export to PDF. Add your logo. Share with your team.
- Free. Forever. Open source. No seat minimums. No paywalls on basics.

That's it. No AI buzzwords. No 47-feature roadmap. Just capture that works.

## Why open source

Scribe raised $30M+ in VC funding. They need $13/seat/month to survive.
They will never make the core free. They structurally can't.

We can. The code is yours. Fork it, self-host it, extend it.
That's not a feature. That's a moat they can never cross.
