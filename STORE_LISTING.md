## Detailed Description
Chingu captures your browser workflows and turns them into clean, shareable step-by-step guides — with screenshots that actually work.

WHY CHINGU?

Every SOP tool today either breaks during capture, paywalls the basics, or charges $13-26/seat/month. Chingu is different:

- Free forever. Not freemium. Free.
- Open source. The code is yours.
- No seat minimums. No hidden paywalls.
- Screenshots that actually work (Scribe's #1 complaint, solved).
- PDF export and branding — free, not locked behind Pro.

FEATURES

Record Mode:
Click "Start Recording", use any website, click "Stop". Get a clean step-by-step guide with screenshots at every click. Smart step descriptions auto-generated from your actions.

Auto-Polish:
Automatically removes duplicate clicks, cleans up descriptions, and generates smart titles. Your guide is polished the moment you stop recording.

Autopilot Mode:
Type step-by-step instructions in plain English. Chingu finds and clicks the right elements automatically, capturing screenshots as it goes.

Export & Share:
One-click PDF export (no print dialog). Add your company logo. Editable step descriptions and titles.
