The problem

Your team forgets everything
the moment someone leaves.

SOPs exist for this. But writing them is brutal. Screenshots, steps, formatting. Nobody has time. So nobody writes them.

How it works

Three steps.
Seriously, just three.

01

Click record.

Hit the Chingu icon and press Start. A floating ● REC bar appears. No popups, no interruptions.

02

Use your app normally.

Every click, every screen, every navigation. Captured automatically.

03

Your guide is ready.

Click Stop in the bar. A toast shows "✅ 5 steps captured → View Guide." Open, annotate, export. Done.
