★ The story 우리 이야기

Built by one person.
For everyone the big tools forgot.

Scribe charges $23 per seat per month. Tango charges $8. Both raised millions and chase Fortune 500 contracts now. Nobody's serving the small team that just wants a clean SOP without filling out an enterprise sales form.

So I built one. Chingu means 친구 in Korean — friend. The product is built around that idea: a friendly tool that does its job, doesn't ask you to sign up, and doesn't try to sell you anything you don't need.

It's open source, MIT licensed, and free forever. Pro buyers fund the cloud features. That's the whole business model. No VC, no growth hacking, no seat minimums.

"If your tool needs a 30-day enterprise trial to use the basics, it's not a tool. It's a sales funnel." — 친구처럼, 도구처럼 (like a friend, like a tool)

Three things. (1) Truly free — Scribe paywalls PDF export and branding, Tango caps you at 15 workflows, MagicHow has a paid team tier. Chingu has zero paywalls. 100% local. All recordings, screenshots, and guides are stored in your browser's local storage using Chrome's chrome.storage API. Nothing is sent to any server. No analytics. No tracking. No login. The optional Cloud sync (Q2, Pro tier) will use end-to-end encryption — server never sees plaintext content.
